jacobian.jacobian(M, model)[source]¶

Parameters:
  • M (vector) – Means for concentrations of species Q, A, R (concatenated)
  • model (struct) – Pre-initialized model struct. See initializeModel.
Returns:

J – System jacobian (linear and nonlinear), scaled by model.dt
